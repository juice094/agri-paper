# Data Sources for Extended Seed Records

The 15 extended seed records were manually curated from well-known, publicly available agricultural extension literature and compendia. No proprietary data was used.

## General References

- USDA National Agricultural Library – Plant Disease Guides
- Extension.org (eXtension) – Crop Pest Management Portals
- APS (American Phytopathological Society) Plant Disease Compendia
- IPM Images and State Extension Fact Sheets (Iowa State, NC State, Penn State, UC IPM, Texas A&M)

## Crop-Specific Sources

### Soybean
- Soybean Rust – USDA-ARS Soybean Rust Information and IPM guides
- Sudden Death Syndrome – NC State Extension Soybean Disease Notes; Iowa State University Extension
- Soybean Cyst Nematode – University of Minnesota Extension; Illinois Extension Nematology

### Potato
- Potato Late Blight – USDA-ARS; Cornell University Plant Disease Clinic fact sheets
- Potato Early Blight – Penn State Extension; University of Idaho Extension
- Common Scab of Potato – Michigan State University Extension; Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada potato disease bulletins

### Cotton
- Cotton Bacterial Blight (Angular Leaf Spot) – Cotton Incorporated; Texas A&M AgriLife Extension
- Verticillium Wilt of Cotton – UC IPM Cotton Guidelines; USDA-ARS Cotton Disease Handbook
- Fusarium Wilt of Cotton – NC State Extension Cotton Portal; Auburn University Plant Pathology

### Peanut
- Early Leaf Spot of Peanut – University of Georgia Extension Peanut Disease Guide
- Late Leaf Spot of Peanut – USDA-ARS National Peanut Research Laboratory; Florida Extension IPM
- Peanut Rust – ICRISAT Groundnut Pathology; Alabama Cooperative Extension System

### Apple
- Apple Scab – Cornell University Fruit IPM; Penn State Tree Fruit Production Guide
- Fire Blight of Apple – Michigan State University Extension; Washington State University Tree Fruit
- Cedar-Apple Rust – Ohio State University Extension; University of Kentucky Plant Pathology fact sheets
